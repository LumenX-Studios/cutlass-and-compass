ABOUT CUTLASS & COMPASS

Where Maritime Heritage Meets Modern Craft

A design and licensing house specializing in maritime heritage and bespoke textile architecture, providing strategic oversight of upcycled natural luxury textiles and maritime-inspired art.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Where Tradition Meets Contemporary Luxury

At Cutlass & Compass, we believe that the traditions of the sea — craftsmanship, resourcefulness, and an eye for enduring beauty — deserve a place in contemporary luxury.

Our work sits at the intersection of heritage storytelling and thoughtful design, bringing maritime-inspired aesthetics to discerning clients and partners around the world.

We operate as a lean, globally minded studio — partnering with artisans, designers, and industry specialists rather than maintaining large-scale infrastructure. This allows us to remain agile, selective, and deeply involved in every project we take on.

WHAT WE DO

Creative Direction Across Complementary Disciplines

Textile Architecture & Upcycled Luxury

We curate and oversee the development of bespoke textile concepts rooted in sustainability and natural materials, giving new life to heritage fabrics and craftsmanship traditions.

Maritime-Inspired Art & Design

From original works to licensed collections, we champion art that draws on seafaring history, coastal landscapes, and nautical iconography.

Culinary Consultancy

We advise private clients, hospitality brands, and super yacht operations on elevated culinary experiences that reflect the same attention to provenance and detail that defines our design work.

OUR APPROACH

Quality, Authenticity, Long-Term Value

We operate as a lean, globally minded studio — partnering with artisans, designers, and industry specialists rather than maintaining large-scale infrastructure.

This allows us to remain agile, selective, and deeply involved in every project we take on. Whether we are advising on a textile collection, licensing a design portfolio, or shaping a culinary programme, our focus is always on quality, authenticity, and long-term value.

LOOKING AHEAD
Cutlass & Compass is built for clients and collaborators who value substance over scale. We remain committed to the principles that started us: a reverence for craft, a respect for the sea, and a belief that good design should tell a story worth remembering.
